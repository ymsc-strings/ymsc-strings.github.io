ADS Seminar

The ADS Seminar is the main public seminar stream for AdS/CFT, quantum gravity, conformal field theory, scattering amplitudes, topological phases, and related mathematical physics. Most 2026 meetings listed by YMSC are in Shuangqing Complex Building A.

For abstracts, Zoom links, and last-minute changes, use the official YMSC ADS seminar page. Email reminders are available via ymscstrings@gmail.com.
