The workshop "Geometric Representation Theory and Quantum Field Theories" is part of the master lecture series workshops to be held at Tsinghua Sanya International Mathematics Forum (TSIMF) for the dates of December 16-20th 2019. Professor Hiraku Nakajima will deliver three master lectures during the workshop, together with talks by other speakers in the related area. TSIMF is at the city of Sanya on the island of Hainan, China, which is famous for its nice weather, beaches and rainforest.

Confirmed Speakers

Tomoyuki Arakawa (RIMS), Ryo Fujita (Kyoto), Sergei Gukov (Caltech), Amihay Hanany (Imperial College), Tatsuyuki Hikita (RIMES), Peter Koroteev (UC Berkeley), Ivan Mirkovic (U Mass, Amherst), Alexei Oblomkov (U Mass, Amherst), Miroslav Rapcak (Perimeter), Valerio Toledano-Laredo (Northeastern), Eric Vasserot (IMJ)

Organizers
