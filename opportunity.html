Opportunities

Postdoctoral Positions

The String Theory group of the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC) at Tsinghua University invites applications for several postdoctoral positions starting from fall of 2020 in the areas of string theory, quantum field theories, mathematical physics, and quantum gravity. The position is for two years with a possibility of extension of another year.

YMSC was established in 2009 and now has more than 80 faculties working in various areas of mathematics. YMSC is at the forefront of research exploring the intersection between physics and mathematics. Current faculty members of the String Theory group include Chi-Ming Chang, Babak Haghighat, Si Li, Hai Lin, Mauricio Romo, Wei Song, Wenbin Yan and Dan Xie. More information about our group can be found at group webpage. YMSC offers very competitive packages, including competitive salaries and benefits, and a generous travel grant to encourage collaboration and interaction with top research centers around the world.

Completed applications must contain curriculum vitae, research statement, selected reprints and/or preprints, as well as three reference letters, which can be submitted through this website. Review of applications will start from December 1 and continue until the positions are filled.
