Postdoctoral Positions

The latest public postdoctoral call for the String Theory group at YMSC was for positions starting in fall 2026. That application round closed on April 30, 2026. Future openings will be posted here when available and through AcademicJobsOnline.

The group works in string theory, quantum field theory, mathematical physics, and quantum gravity. Current faculty members in these areas include Chi-Ming Chang, Babak Haghighat, Ling-Yan Hung, Si Li, Wei Song, Ning Su, Junya Yagi, and Wenbin Yan.

For reference, the fall 2026 posting is archived at AcademicJobsOnline.
