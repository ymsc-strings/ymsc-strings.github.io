Visitor Information

Tsinghua University and YMSC

The campus of Tsinghua University is situated in northwest Beijing on the site of the former imperial gardens of the Qing Dynasty, and surrounded by a number of historical sites. The campus is roughly bounded by ZhongGuanCun North Street and HeQing Road west and east, and by HeQing Road and ChengFu Road north and south. The campus area sizes 442.12 hectare.
Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC) is currently located in three buildings near the west gate of the university: JingZhai, JinChunYuan West Building, and NingZhai. The campus map is HERE.

Getting to the University

From Beijing Capital International Airport (BJS), the simplest way to the university is by taxi. Please be ware of taxi scams at BJS and take licensed taxi only. There are also subway and buses to the university as well. Detailed transportation guide can be found HERE.

Internet Access

Any faculty can help you get the guest account from Guest Tsinghua. Notice that the link is accessible only in Tsinghua and you need a valid e-mail address to receive the password.
According to Chinese law and regulations, access to certain websites and web services is restricted. It is advised to plan ahead before your visit (setting up a vpn account, forwarding your gmail to another e-mail account, and etc).
