Informal Seminars / Family Talks

Family Talks are informal internal discussions of the string theory group. They are announced through the group mailing list and internal channels when scheduled, rather than maintained as a public fixed-term calendar.

To receive announcements, subscribe to ymscstrings@gmail.com with subject "subscribe" and your name and institution in the body.
