Research Summary

Bin Gui studies mathematical structures in two-dimensional conformal field theory, especially the relations among vertex operator algebras, conformal nets, and tensor categories. He uses tools from functional analysis, von Neumann algebras, and subfactor theory to study unitary VOAs and their module categories, aiming to understand the correspondence between algebraic and analytic models of conformal field theory.
