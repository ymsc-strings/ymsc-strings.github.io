Research Summary

Ling-Yan Hung connects holography, topological quantum field theory, tensor networks, and topological phases. Her papers over the past five years continue to develop holographic pictures relating p-adic CFT, tensor networks, and emergent geometry, while also systematically studying symmetry-preserving renormalization-group flows, anyon condensation, and critical lattice models in SymTFT and boundary theories of topological order. This work places emergent spacetime in quantum gravity and topological order/categorical symmetries in condensed matter and mathematical physics into a common language.
