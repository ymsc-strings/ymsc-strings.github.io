Research Summary

Wenbin Yan studies exact structures in supersymmetric field theory, especially correspondences among 4d N=2 SCFTs, Argyres-Douglas theories, class S constructions, Schur indices, and vertex operator algebras. Over the past five years, his work has continued to develop 4d/2d and 4d/3d mirror relations, the classification of low-rank N=2 SCFTs, mirror symmetry after compactification, instanton counting in M-theory, and matches between VOA representation theory and the geometry of Hitchin moduli spaces.
