Sarthak Duary

Postdoctoral Fellow, Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University

Basic Information

Email
sarthakduary (at) tsinghua.edu.cn
Office
Shuangqing Complex Building C657

Research Areas

String theory, the AdS/CFT correspondence, black-hole microstates, and flat-space holography.

Education

  • 2018.08-2024.08, Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics, ICTS-TIFR, Bengaluru, India.
  • 2016.07-2018.07, M.S. in Physics, IIT Kharagpur.
  • 2013.09-2016.06, B.S. in Physics, Ramakrishna Mission Residential College Narendrapur.

Research Summary

Sarthak Duary works on string theory and holographic duality, focusing on black-hole microstates, AdS/CFT, and relations between field-theoretic and gravitational descriptions in flat-space holography.
