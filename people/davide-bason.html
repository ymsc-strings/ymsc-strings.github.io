Davide Bason

Postdoctoral Fellow, Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University

Basic Information

Email
davidebason (at) tsinghua.edu.cn

Research Areas

Four-dimensional supersymmetric gauge theories with boundaries and string-theoretic constructions of gauge theories.

Education

  • 2021-2024, Ph.D., University of Trieste.
  • 2019-2021, M.S., University of Turin.
  • 2016-2019, B.S., University of Turin.

Research Summary

Davide Bason studies supersymmetric gauge theories and their string-theoretic realizations, with particular interest in how boundary conditions, defects, and geometric engineering reveal dualities and exact structures in field theory.

Representative Publications

  • D. Bason, M. Billo, “theta-diagram technique for N = 1, d=4 superfields,” Eur. Phys. J. C 83 (2023).
  • D. Bason, L. Di Pietro, R. Valandro, J. van Muiden, “BCFT One-point Functions of Coulomb Branch Operators,” JHEP 08 (2024) 210.
