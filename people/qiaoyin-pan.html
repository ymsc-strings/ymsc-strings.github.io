Qiaoyin Pan (潘俏尹)

Postdoctoral Fellow, Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University

Basic Information

Email
qpan (at) tsinghua.edu.cn
Office
Shuangqing Complex Building A515

Research Areas

Quantum gravity, topological quantum field theory, and quantum groups.

Education

  • 2013-2017, B.S., Nankai University.
  • 2017-2018, M.S., University of Waterloo.
  • 2018-2022, Ph.D., University of Waterloo.

Research Summary

Qiaoyin Pan connects quantum gravity, topological field theory, and quantum algebra, focusing on applications of topological structures and quantum-group methods to low-dimensional field theories and gravitational questions.
