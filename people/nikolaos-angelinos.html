Research Summary

Nikolaos Angelinos studies quantum information, conformal field theory, and holography, with a recent emphasis on the role of error-correcting codes and ensemble structures. His work on Narain CFTs, WZW models, stabilizer states, and Abelian three-dimensional TQFT gravity explores how discrete code data can organize families of conformal theories and their gravitational duals. Related projects on Krylov-space diagnostics and long-range fermion entanglement use information-theoretic tools to probe dynamics and correlations beyond conventional local observables.
