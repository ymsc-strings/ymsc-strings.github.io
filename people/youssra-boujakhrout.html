Research Areas

Mathematical physics and integrability in gauge theory and string theory.

Education

  • 2019.12-2023.09, Ph.D. in Mathematical Physics, Mohammed V University in Rabat.
  • 2017.09-2019.09, M.S. in Mathematical Physics, Mohammed V University in Rabat.
  • 2014.09-2017.07, B.S. in Physical Sciences, Mohammed First University, Oujda.

Research Summary

Youssra Boujakhrout studies applications of integrable systems in gauge theory and string theory, focusing on exact structures, spectral problems, and how integrability organizes computable quantities in quantum field theory and string theory.

Representative Publications

  • Y. Boujakhrout et al., “Superspin chains solutions from 4D Chern-Simons theory,” JHEP 2024 (4).
  • Y. Boujakhrout et al., “'t Hooft lines of ADE-type and topological quivers,” SciPost Phys. 15 (2023) 078.
  • Y. Boujakhrout et al., “Embedding integrable superspin chain in string theory,” Nucl. Phys. B 990 (2023) 116156.
  • Y. Boujakhrout et al., “Lax operator and superspin chains from 4D CS gauge theory,” J. Phys. A 55 (2022) 415402.
