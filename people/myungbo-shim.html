Myungbo Shim

Postdoctoral Fellow, Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University

Basic Information

Email
mbshim (at) tsinghua.edu.cn
Office

Research Areas

String theory, supersymmetric field theory, topological field theory, vertex operator algebras, and the 3d-3d correspondence.

Education

  • 2011.03-2015.02, B.S. in Physics, Kyung Hee University.
  • 2015.05-2017.02, M.S. in Physics, Kyung Hee University.
  • 2017.03-2022.02, Ph.D. in Physics, Kyung Hee University.

Research Summary

Myungbo Shim studies topological structures in string theory and supersymmetric field theory, especially relations among the 3d-3d correspondence, vertex operator algebras, and topological field theory.
