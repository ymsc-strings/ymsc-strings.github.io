Research Areas

Theoretical physics, quantum gravity, quantum field theory, and holographic duality.

Research Summary

Pengxiang Hao studies three-dimensional flat-space, Carrollian, Galilean, and BMS holography, where the usual relativistic conformal symmetry is replaced by non-Lorentzian symmetry algebras. His recent work develops bulk reconstruction in Flat3/CCFT2 and relates it to flat limits of (A)dS3/CFT2, while parallel projects analyze BMS-invariant free fields, modular Hamiltonians, entanglement entropy, and Carrollian-Galilean conformal blocks. Work on AdS/BCFT, de Sitter islands, and glue-on AdS for TTbar-deformed CFTs further connects these nonstandard holographic limits to entanglement and boundary-gravity constructions.
