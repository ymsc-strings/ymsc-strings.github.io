Research Summary

Bartek Czech studies how quantum information can give rise to spacetime and gravity, primarily in the setting of AdS/CFT. His work uses entanglement entropy, kinematic space, bit threads, tensor networks, modular Hamiltonians, and Berry phases to describe bulk geometry, with particular emphasis on how boundary CFT data encodes bulk curves, regions, and transport structures.
