Research Areas

Employment

  • 2023-present, Member, Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University.
  • 2019-2023, Postdoctoral Fellow, Stanford University.

Research Summary

Zhenbin Yang works on quantum gravity and AdS/CFT, especially quantum chaos, black-hole interiors, and the role of wormholes in the black-hole information problem. His earlier work includes the duality between JT gravity and the SYK model, conformal-symmetry breaking near AdS2, traversable wormholes, and quantum many-body teleportation; more recently he has focused on quantum chaos in black-hole interiors, supersymmetric black holes, and de Sitter quantum gravity.
