Research Summary

Qingrui Wang studies topological phases of matter and their mathematical structures, especially symmetry-protected topological phases in interacting fermion systems, topological quantum field theory, loop-braiding statistics, and low-dimensional boundary theories. His work connects solvable models and field-theoretic language in condensed matter theory with mathematical tools such as group cohomology and modular tensor categories.
