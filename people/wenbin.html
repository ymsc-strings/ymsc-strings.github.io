About me

I spent my undergraduate at Tsinghua University from 2003 to 2007. Then I pursued the Ph.D. at YITP, Stony Brook University under the advice of professor Leonardo Rastelli.
After graduating from Stony Brook in 2012, I spent three years at Caltech Theory Group as a postdoctoral researcher (Sherman-Fairchild fellow). After that I was a postdoctoral research at CMSA, Harvard University> from 2015 to 2017.
In 2017, I joined the Yau Mathematical Science Center, Tsinghua University as a faculty member.
