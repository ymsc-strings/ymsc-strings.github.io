Rongge Xu (许容阁)

Postdoctoral Fellow, Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University

Basic Information

Email
rongge (at) tsinghua.edu.cn
Office
Shuangqing Complex Building B519

Research Areas

Many-body quantum systems, quantum gravity, topological phases, category theory, and higher algebra.

Education

  • 2013-2017, B.S., University of Minnesota Twin Cities.
  • 2018-2025, Ph.D., Fudan University.

Research Summary

Rongge Xu studies structural connections among many-body quantum systems, topological phases, and quantum gravity, using category theory and higher algebra to describe topological and quantum-information features.

Representative Publications

  • L. Guo, Y. Yan, R. Xu, J. Li, C. Zeng, “Zero-Bias Conductance Peaks Effectively Tuned by Gating-Controlled Rashba Spin-Orbit Coupling,” Phys. Rev. Lett. 126 (2021) 057701.
  • R. Xu, Z.-H. Zhang, “Categorical Descriptions of 1-dimensional Gapped Phases with Abelian Onsite Symmetries,” Phys. Rev. B 110 (2024) 155106.
  • R. Xu, H. Yang, “2-Morita Equivalent Condensable Algebras in Topological Orders,” Chinese Phys. Lett. 42 (2025) 070710.
