Research Summary

Robert McRae studies algebraic structures arising in two-dimensional conformal quantum field theory, especially vertex operator algebras, affine Lie algebras, Virasoro algebras, and their module categories. His work asks when these module categories carry tensor, braided, and modular tensor category structures, and applies them to rationality questions in logarithmic conformal field theory and orbifold theory.
