Research Areas

Research Summary

Ning Su uses bootstrap methods as a central tool to constrain and solve strongly coupled quantum field theories without relying on Lagrangian perturbation theory. Over the past five years, his work has covered high-precision data for the 3d Ising and super-Ising models, numerical algorithms such as the navigator function, non-Abelian current bootstrap, hybrid analytic lightcone and numerical bootstrap methods, and S-matrix bootstrap fits to pion-scattering data. These works advance numerical precision in conformal field theory and extend bootstrap methods to scattering amplitudes and strong-interaction phenomena.
