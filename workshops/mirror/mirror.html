About the Event

We are having a special year on "symplectic dualities and three dimensional mirror symmetry here at YMSC, Tsinghua University during the 2019-2020 academic year. We will arrange talks, lectures and mini-workshops from mathematicians and physicists working on related topics, so people from both side can learn from each other and work together. This event is organized by Peng Shan, Dan Xie and Wenbin Yan.
